आपको महा शिवरात्रि की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं! भगवान शिव आपको सुख, शांति और समृद्धि से आशीर्वाद दें। उत्साह से त्योहार मनाएं!    ( May Lord Shiva bless you with happiness, peace, and prosperity. Enjoy the festive celebrations!)

Maha Shivratri on 8th March

On this day, It is believed that Bhagwan Shiv and Devi Parwati got married. And also, Bhagwan Shiv performed Tandav(Basically a dance of creation, preservation, and destruction). Shivratri is observed on the fourteenth day of the dark (waning) half of the lunar month of Phalguna or Magha. It is also known as "Great night of Shiv". The celebration includes "Jaagran", an all night prayer. Offering of fruit, leaves, sweets, and milk are made to lord Shiva, and many people also do all day fast to worship him.
